What is PFQF?

Public Fund Questioning Forum Nepal (PFQF Nepal) is a non-governmental organization committed to promoting transparency, accountability, and good governance in Nepal. PFQF Nepal has some specific objectives through which it wants to promote citizen's participation and surveillance over the public funds and its spending.
