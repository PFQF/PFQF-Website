Our Objectives

PFQF Nepal aim to achieve

Public Awareness:

We educate and raise awareness among citizens about their rights to question on public funds, encouraging active civic engagement.

Transparency Advocacy:

We work to increase transparency in public spending, advocating for easily accessible and comprehensive financial information.

Capacity Building:

We conduct capacity development training and resources to individuals, community-based organizations, civil society organizations, and local communities to enhance their ability to scrutinize public expenditures effectively.

Collaborative Partnerships:

We collaborate with government bodies, NGOs, and international organizations to promote a culture of openness and integrity.
